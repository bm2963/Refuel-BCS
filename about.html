About Refuel BCS

Built for busy days in the Brazos Valley.

Refuel BCS is a mobile fuel delivery service designed to help you skip the gas station. We’re focused on convenience, clear communication, and a smooth, professional experience.

Meet the Team

Refuel BCS was founded by Christopher Braly and Christopher Lamb , two lifelong first responders with more than 50 years of combined emergency service experience. With decades spent protecting and serving the Brazos Valley, they built Refuel BCS on the same principles that guided their careers... safety, reliability, and trust.

By bringing fuel directly to homes, businesses, and job sites, Refuel BCS delivers a safer, more convenient fueling solution that is proudly owned and operated right here in the community it serves.

Our Story

Our story begins right here in the Brazos Valley. As local first responders, we’ve spent our careers serving this community when it matters most—responding to emergencies, protecting families, and helping neighbors through their toughest moments. That experience shaped who we are and how we approach everything we do.

We started Refuel BCS to build a reputable, locally owned mobile fueling service that people can truly count on. Too often, convenience comes at the cost of service or accountability. We believe it doesn’t have to. By staying local, hiring responsibly, and operating with integrity, our goal is to build long-term relationships with individuals, families, and businesses throughout the Brazos Valley.

At its core, Refuel BCS is about safety and convenience. Whether you’re fueling a personal vehicle, equipment, generators, or a fleet, traveling to a gas station isn’t always practical—or safe. By bringing fuel directly to you, we help reduce unnecessary trips, time away from work or family, and exposure to late-night or roadside fueling risks.

This business isn’t just about delivering fuel. It’s about continuing to serve our neighbors with the same professionalism, reliability, and commitment that guided our years in public service—now in a new way, one delivery at a time.

Our Mission & Values

What we’re building — and what guides how we operate.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make refueling as simple and stress-free as possible by delivering fuel directly to where vehicles are parked. We aim to remove unnecessary stops, save time, and help customers stay focused on their day—whether they’re managing busy schedules, caring for family, or running a business.

Refuel BCS is built around consistency, professionalism, and clear communication. From the moment a request is made to the completion of service, our goal is to provide a smooth, reliable experience that customers can trust and comfortably use again.

Our Values

  • Safety-first: We follow a consistent process and put safety ahead of speed.
  • Clear communication: Customers should always know what to expect and when.
  • Respect your time: We’re built to reduce friction—not add more steps.
  • Professional service: Clean, courteous, and reliable delivery every time.

Commitment to Safety & Standards

Mobile refueling should feel just as responsible as it is convenient. Our approach is built around consistency, safe handling, and clear expectations.

Consistent process

We use a repeatable step-by-step workflow for each delivery—confirming location details, verifying the request, and completing service with a clean finish and confirmation.

Safe delivery practices

Deliveries are performed with an emphasis on safety at every step—proper positioning, attention to the environment, and careful handling to reduce risk and maintain a professional standard.

Customer clarity

We keep expectations simple and transparent—what’s needed, where service can occur, and how customers can reach us if anything changes.

As Refuel BCS grows, we’ll continue refining training, equipment readiness, and documented procedures to keep the experience consistent and trustworthy.

Local Commitment

We’re building Refuel BCS with a local-first mindset—serving the Brazos Valley well, listening to customers, and improving the experience over time.

Serving our community

Refuel BCS is committed to serving the Brazos Valley with a local-first mindset. We believe great service comes from understanding the needs of the community we operate in and showing up consistently with professionalism and care.

Our focus is on building long-term trust through dependable service, clear expectations, and responsiveness. As we grow, we plan to listen closely to customer feedback and continue improving the experience to better serve individuals, families, and local businesses throughout the area.

Service Area

We currently focus on College Station, Bryan, and nearby Brazos Valley areas. Coverage and availability will expand based on demand and operational readiness.
