Fueling made simple—without the station.

We’re preparing to launch later this year. Here’s how one-time fueling and subscriptions will work at launch.

Two ways to use Refuel BCS

  • One-time fueling — request fuel when you need it
  • Subscription plan — set a schedule and stay topped off

Final plan options will be announced at launch.

One-time fueling

Request

Select your fuel type, location, and preferred time window (via the app at launch).

Deliver

Our technician arrives, fuels your vehicle, and provides a digital receipt.

Subscription plans

A great option for routines: commuting, family vehicles, or small fleets.

Set a schedule

Choose a recurring day/time window that fits your week.

Stay topped off

No more “I forgot to get gas” moments.

Easy management

Update vehicles, preferences, and notifications (app-based at launch).

Rules for fueling

Private property

Vehicle should be parked on private property (driveway, approved lot, or company location).

No enclosed spaces

Avoid garages or enclosed areas so the vehicle can be accessed safely.

Fuel door access

Please ensure your fuel door is unlocked/accessible for a quick, smooth service.

Full safety policies and service rules will be published at launch.

How we come up with pricing

Fuel cost

Fuel is priced based on current market rates and fuel type.

Delivery/service fee

A service fee covers delivery, equipment, and time—so you don’t have to detour to a station.

Exact fees and subscription pricing will be announced closer to launch.

Why mobile fueling is safer & better

Less exposure

Skip station crowds and late-night stops when you can fuel at home or work.

Professional process

Consistent procedures, proper equipment, and a safety-first approach.

Convenience

Fuel delivered to you—no lines, no detours, no wasted time.
