How It Works

Four simple steps to get refueled without a gas station trip.

1

Download the Refuel BCS App

Download the Refuel BCS app to request fuel, choose your fuel type, and select a delivery location and time.

The app is the fastest way to manage requests and receive updates.

2

Park Your Vehicle

Park on private property—such as your home driveway, a private parking lot, or an approved company location.

Avoid enclosed garages so our technician can access your vehicle safely.

3

We Deliver the Fuel

Our certified technician arrives at your location, fuels your vehicle with care, and confirms when the service is complete.

4

Drive Away Full

No lines, no station stops—just a full tank delivered directly to you.

FAQ

Is mobile refueling safe?

Yes — when performed with proper equipment and consistent procedures. Safety is built into the process.

Do I need to be present?

Sometimes. If we need access info (gates, lots, parking), being available by phone is usually enough.

Where can you refuel my vehicle?

Typically on private property (driveways, private lots, approved workplace locations).

How fast can you get here?

Same-day availability is common. Timing depends on demand and your location.
