What to Expect at Launch

The goal is a smooth, simple flow: request fuel, get confirmation, and come back to a full tank. Here’s what the early experience is designed to look like.

Simple requests

Submit a request with your location, fuel type, and timing preferences. We’ll confirm the details before delivery so there are no surprises.

Clear updates

You’ll receive basic status updates (requested → confirmed → on the way → complete) so you always know what’s happening.

Professional finish

Deliveries are completed with a consistent process. After service, you’ll have confirmation that your vehicle was refueled and ready to go.

Early access notes

Launch availability may start limited while routes and operations ramp up. If your area isn’t covered right away, we’ll keep expanding based on demand.

First-Time Delivery Checklist

These quick basics help make delivery fast and clean — especially during early rollout.

What to have ready

  • Accurate location: address + helpful notes (gate code, building, parking level).
  • Vehicle access: fuel door accessible and not blocked.
  • Parked in a suitable spot: open-air, safe area with room to work.
  • Phone reachable: in case we need a quick clarification.

Good delivery locations

  • Home driveways (open-air)
  • Private apartment / business lots (where permitted)
  • Approved workplace locations
  • Other private property with safe access

If you’re not sure a spot works, reach out first and we’ll help you pick a good location.

Is This a Fit for You?

Refuel BCS is built for convenience — but a few conditions help us keep service consistent.

Great fit if you…

  • Want to skip station stops during busy weeks
  • Have a predictable parking spot at home or work
  • Prefer clear communication and a simple process
  • Need occasional top-offs for multiple vehicles

May not be a fit if…

  • Your vehicle is always parked in an enclosed garage
  • Access to the vehicle is restricted (no entry, no permission)
  • You need a service outside the current coverage area

Coverage and rules may evolve as the service expands.

Launch FAQ

Questions specifically about rollout and early availability. (The main FAQ is on the Home page.)

When will the app be available?

Launch timing is being finalized. Email us for the most accurate status and early availability details.

Will service be available in both Bryan and College Station?

That’s the focus area. We’ll expand coverage based on demand and operational readiness.

How do I request fuel without the app?

During early rollout, you can contact us by email/phone and we’ll confirm details manually.

What details will you need from me?

Location, fuel type, timing preference, and any access notes (gate codes, parking level, etc.).

Will pricing be listed in the app?

That’s the plan. Pricing will be transparent and based on fuel type and delivery needs.
